## About
City Transportation Systems (CTS) was created to simplify and optimize the management of public transport in the city.
The project combines a number of functional features aimed at ensuring the convenience and efficiency of travel for citizens.
CTS provides citizens with many functions aimed at improving the quality of public transport.
Users can easily get up-to-date information about routes, schedules and stops, which allows them to plan their trips effectively.
CTS was created with a focus on ensuring passenger comfort. User-friendly interfaces, informative maps and accurate schedules make trips enjoyable and predictable.
CTS is a key element of an intelligent city, contributing to sustainable development and improving the quality of life of citizens. 
Modern technologies and analytics used in CTS allow the city to adapt to growing needs and effectively manage public transport.
